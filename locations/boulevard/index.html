Boulevard/Glenwood Crossing Concept

Phase 1: Glenwood Ave could be connected to the Boulevard intersection north of the firestation. Traffic lights could be updated to allow for a direct bike path crossing at Glenwood. Access to Boulevard from a new traffic light on an eastbound Glenwood Ave would reduce traffic on Sydney by Dakota Blue and reduce the frequency of accidents at the crossing by CVS which has no traffic light.


Phase 2: GDOT adds left turn freeway-exit route using half of oneway streatch of Glenwood Ave connected to the improved Boulevard intersection.

1. Allows freeway traffic to continue straight onto Glenwood Ave heading east toward Kroger, thereby reducing cars turning east at CVS to use neighborhood backstreets.

2. Reduces vehicle traffic along the Beltline on busy Glenwood Park side of Kroger.

3. Adding a left turn from westbound Glenwood Avenue to southbound Boulevard reduces traffic on neighborhood streets east of Boulevard.

4. Aligns crosswalk with a new protected multiuse bike path on Glenwood Ave from Boulevard to MHJ High School and the Beltline.
